**momentum(운동량)**은 “상태 변수(state variable)”
**force(힘)**는 “그 상태를 바꾸는 원인(change operator)”
**moment(모멘트)**는 “기준점/축 O를 도입해서, 선형 정보를 회전 관점으로 바꾸는 연산(about O)”이에요.


---


In his Principia, Newton used the Latin phrase motus (motion) to describe $mv$. He formalized "Quantity of Motion" as a conservation law—that in a closed system, the total "amount of moving" doesn't change.

They borrowed "moment" from the Principle of the Lever (Archimedes’ old "turning weight"). They realized that "distance from the center" was just as important as the force itself, leading to the "Moment of Force" (Torque) and "Moment of Inertia" (rotational mass).

moment of force:τ=r×F (토크 torque)
moment of momentum: L=r×p (각운동량 angular momentum)
